CoVid'19 Automatic Phase Detection

Idea Summary

At present, the spread of COVID-19 is shown to have an exponential trend in most countries. An exponential curve is difficult to understand and adopt for comparision among different regions.This project was developed to automatically break the pandemic spread into multiple phases.The software is available for World, India and detailed statistics are available for districts of India as well.

Description

As of 19 July 2020, more than 14.2 million cases have been reported across 188 countries and territories, resulting in more than 600,000 deaths.This does not end here.This is a lot of information.We tried to break this information among different regions, in diffrent phases.At this time, we use a knee detection algorithm to find the different phases for the pandemic graph.This algorithm breaks the exponential curve into a multi-linear curve, which gives us the rate of the spread for the disease. The data is useful for comparing phases of spread across different regions.

P Biswas, KPS Saluja, S Arjun, LRD Murthy, G Prabhakar, VK Sharma and Jeevitha Shree DV, COVID 19 Data Visualization through Automatic Phase Detection, ACM Digital Government: Research and Practice (DGOV) 2020
