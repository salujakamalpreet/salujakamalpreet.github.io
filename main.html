Kamalpreet Singh Saluja

Man device_hub Machine

Observe, Design, Code, and Be Quite.

Research Assistant at I3D Lab , CPDM under Dr. Pradipta Biswas at Indian Institute of Science(IISc), Bangalore.

I am working in various projects which tend to fill the voids between Man Machine Interaction. I am presently working on design and development of gaze controlled applications for e-Inclusion of users with Severe Speech and Motor Impairment(SSMI) under Dr. Pradipta Biswas. I am also involved in design and development of Smart Sensor Dashboard in Virtual Reality. Previously, I worked at IDeAS Lab at CPDM under Dr. Amaresh Chakraborty at IISc, Bangalore. The project was about designing a Mutually Coordinated Interactive visualization platform tool named “SweetSpot” for Product Life Cycle Assesment.

I believe in intuitive freedom to interact, to communicate with machines, thereby reducing any further friction. My focus is to develop similar paradigm in the area of Virtual and Augmented Reality.
