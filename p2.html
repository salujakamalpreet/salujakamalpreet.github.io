Cognitive and Auditory Assesment Kit

Funded By DBT , Govt. of India

We designed a assesment and training kit to put in equation , relationship between auditory and mild cognitive impairment and find utilities of auditory and cognitive training for rehabilitation.

Idea Summary

According to industry estimate, by 2018, more than 50 percent of 3.4 billion smartphone and tablet users will have downloaded mobile health applications. We tend to leverage existing software platform provided by smartphones and tablets to develop a software application for cognitive and auditory assesment of users.

Description

Aging is known to contribute to progressive deterioration of the central nervous system functioning. These changes are characterized by slowing of perceptual information processing, thereby cognitive decline
There exists test such as Montreal Cognitive Assesment(MoCA) or and Mini-Mental State Exam(MMSE), but there is hardly a free version which makes it to be evaulated online. We aim to digitise and make the process automatic.Also, the tests are available in multiple Indian Languages.

Access to Cognitive and Auditory Assesment kit

Cognitive Assesment Test

The Cognitive Test battery consists of TMT, N-Back, Naming and Clock Drawing tests. Data can be collected for any subset of tests. The TMT test can be configured for new language. The N-Back test supports 1 and 2-back tests for both words and images. The Clock drawing test is integrated to automatic sketch recognition feature.

Speech Perception Test

The speech perception test comprises of a set of equally intelligible 150 sentences following this paper (MacLeod, A. and Summerfield, Q. (1990), “A procedure for measuring auditory and audio visual speech- reception thresholds for sentences in noise: rationale, evaluation, and recommendations for use”, British Journal of Audiology). It has recorded voice from a native British speaker, TTS support and instantaneous feedback feature.
