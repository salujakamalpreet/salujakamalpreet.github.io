Visualization: Life Cycle Assesment(LCA)

Idea Summary

Early 20% of design activities decide nearly 80% of the potential environmental impact caused by the product. Life cycle assessment (LCA) tools fail to provide adequate understanding of data which results in poor interpretation of results for designers.We developed a software interface which generates interactive visual narratives of Life cycle assessment data to aid designers to reason about environmental impacts of existing products.

How did I Contribute?

  • Developed Visualisations
  • Developed a plugin to OpenLCA(open-source LCA software)
  • UI/UX Design

Description

OpenLCA is an open source and free software for Sustainability and Life Cycle Assessment.It plays in the same league as commercial LCA software, such as SimaPro, GaBi, or Umberto. We aim to provide them an insightful experience, which allows them to counteract the limitations of interpreting complex data.The tool generates interactive visual narratives of life cycle assessment data.
